What is Tapas?

A tapa is an appetizer or snack in Spanish cuisine. Tapas may be cold or hot. In some bars and restaurants in Spain and across the globe, tapas have evolved into a more sophisticated cuisine. Tapas can be combined to make a full meal.

Where's the best place to eat tapas?

This depends, but different areas are known for different styles of tapa. for instance, in the north of Spain near San Sebastian and Bilbao people eat small plates in pinxos. These tend to be smaller plates of several elements that are held together using cocktail sticks. In turn, these cocktail sticks are used when figuring out how much you have to pay.
Whereas in Andalucia in the south of Spain, tapa plates tend to be larger and more varied in their contents.
